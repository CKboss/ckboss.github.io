Tensorflow初体验

安装:

个人感觉Tensoflow是最容易安装的.

tensorflow0.11_rc1已经可以支持cuda8.在ubuntu16.04下安装更加方便

介绍一下在Ubuntu16.04下安装带有GPU的tensorflow:

安装总共需要4个文件:

CUDA8.0: 这个可以在官网上下到,并且包含了Nvidia显卡的驱动,也就是说并不需要先安装显卡驱动

  • cuda-repo-ubuntu1604-8-0-local_8.0.44-1_amd64.deb
    cudnn5.1: 这个需要先注册一下再从官网上下载
  • cudnn-8.0-linux-x64-v5.1.tgz
    anacoda: Pychon全家桶
  • Anaconda3-4.2.0-Linux-x86_64.sh
    tensoflow的安装包,因为谷歌被墙最好先翻墙下好.
  • tensorflow-0.11.0rc1-cp35-cp35m-linux_x86_64.whl

安装步骤:

  1. 先安装cuda,这个参照官网的安装教程就可以了

默认会安装在/usr/local/cuda下面,同时也会安装上显卡的驱动.
安装显卡驱动的过程可能有些折腾…
能看到这个界面就对了:

如果没有,慢慢折腾驱动吧…

  1. 安装cudnn
    下载后解压,有一个lib64和include的文件. 把这两个文件夹里的内容复制到cuda中对应的位置.
    注意cp的时候用带上-p选项,复制完以后ldconfig一下.
  2. 安装anacoda
    没啥好说的运行下那个.sh文件就可以了,完成后系统的默认python就被换掉了.
  3. 安装tensorflow

至此tensorflow应该就安装好了,如果不想折腾gpu可以安装cpu版的tensorflow略去1,2步,直接安装anacoda,pip安装对应cpu版本的tensorflow就可了(很慢…)
更多见:
https://www.tensorflow.org/versions/r0.11/get_started/index.html

  1. feed_dict给placehold变量赋值

HDOJ 5858 Hard problem 积分

博客恢复更新了,顺便看了下今年多校的题目…

Hard problem

Problem Description

cjj is fun with math problem. One day he found a Olympic Mathematics problem for primary school students. It is too difficult for cjj. Can you solve it?

Give you the side length of the square L, you need to calculate the shaded area in the picture.

The full circle is the inscribed circle of the square, and the center of two quarter circle is the vertex of square, and its radius is the length of the square.

Input

The first line contains a integer T(1<=T<=10000), means the number of the test case. Each case contains one line with integer l(1<=l<=10000).

Output

For each test case, print one line, the shade area in the picture. The answer is round to two digit.

Sample Input

1
1

Sample Output

0.29

Author

BUPT

Source

2016 Multi-University Training Contest 10

如何计算阴影部分的面积还是很显然的.把原图分成两半,只看一边的话,先计算出小圆和大圆的交点,然后用积分分两段算出交点下方的面积就可以了.
主要是半圆的定积分的计算比较麻烦,求出的式子比较长…

$\frac{x \left(L^2-3 L x+2 L \sqrt{x (L-x)}+2 x^2\right)-L^2 \sqrt{x} \sqrt{L-x} \tan ^{-1}\left(\frac{\sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{L-x}}\right)}{4 \sqrt{x (L-x)}}$

$\frac{2 L^2 \sqrt{x} \sqrt{2 L-x} \tan ^{-1}\left(\frac{\sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{2 L-x}}\right)-x \left(2 L^2-3 L x+x^2\right)}{2 \sqrt{x (2 L-x)}}$
